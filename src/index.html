DivvyUP!

Divvy is Chicago's bike sharing program. The data for Divvy's ridership is publicly available. I took data for Divvy Bike trips from May 17th, 2016, and created an animation using the D3 library and Google Maps API. For visualization purposes, I assumed that every trip followed the recommended cycling directions from Google Maps.

Pause time and double click on timebar to jump to a different part of the day!
